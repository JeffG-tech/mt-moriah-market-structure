Mount Moriah Posting Guide

(For Facebook & Social Media Captions)

1. Tone & Voice

  • Professional yet approachable. Speak like you’re guiding a family through a big decision, not like a street vendor shouting a sale.
  • Faith-driven & legacy-focused. Emphasize community, family, ownership, and long-term security.
  • Informative, not exaggerated. Avoid hype. Share facts, features, and benefits.

2. Caption Formula (Safe Template)

Every post should follow this flow:

  1. Hook (Friendly, Positive Statement)
    Example: “Owning land is one of the strongest ways to secure your family’s future.”
  2. Property/Service Details
    • Location
    • Lot area / size
    • Key features (road access, flood-free, nearby schools, etc.)
  3. Benefit (Value without hype words)
    “Flexible terms available” instead of “Zero downpayment”
    “Safe and transparent process” instead of “No risk guaranteed”
  4. Call to Action
    “Message us for full details”
    “Schedule a site viewing today”
    “We’d love to guide you through the process”

3. Red Flag Words to Avoid

(Never include these in captions)

  • “Guaranteed profit / sure profit / get rich quick”
  • “Lot for sale” (use “Property opportunity” or “Lot available”)
  • “Act NOW!!! / Hurry before it’s gone!!!”
  • “Risk-free investment”
  • “Cash advance / loan / instant approval”
  • “Tag and share to win / click this now”

4. Safe Alternatives to Use

  • Instead of “For Sale” → “Open for ownership” / “Available lot”
  • Instead of “Guaranteed” → “Designed for” / “Built for”
  • Instead of “Hot deal” → “Special offer”
  • Instead of “Don’t miss this!!!” → “Reserve your slot today”
  • Instead of “No risk” → “Safe and transparent process”

5. Image & Content Rules

  • Always use real site photos, maps, or happy clients.
  • Avoid overly edited “dream mansion” stock photos.
  • No suggestive, violent, or misleading images.
  • Keep posts simple: 1–2 pictures with a clean caption.

6. Posting Rhythm

  • Limit to 1–2 posts per day. Flooding the page looks spammy.
  • Mix content:
    • Educational (titling process, steps to own land)
    • Inspirational (quotes on family & legacy)
    • Property highlights (lot availability, subdivision updates)
    • Client wins (turnovers, title releases)

7. Sample Safe Caption

“Another step towards family legacy!
Project Isaac now has a 200sqm lot available at Block 1, Lot 5.
Accessible location, peaceful environment, and flexible terms.
Secure your property today. Message us to schedule a site viewing.”

When to Avoid “Investment”

Kapag may kasamang promises:

  • “Guaranteed investment returns”
  • “Sure profit investment”
  • “No risk investment”

Kapag tone ay parang quick-money scheme.

Safe Use of “Investment”

Pwede pa rin gamitin basta:

  • Context is long-term, family, or legacy.
    Example: “Owning land is a wise investment for your family’s future.”
  • “Real estate remains one of the most stable investments in the Philippines.”

Iwasan ang money-talk style. Instead, make it values-based.

Best Practice

Kung ayaw mo ng risk na ma-flag, gamitin safer alternatives:

  • “Property ownership”
  • “Securing your family’s future”
  • “Long-term value”
  • “Building a lasting legacy”
  • “Safe and transparent process”

Para hindi restrictive ang posts ng agents, magandang rule:

Use "investment" sparingly, and only in an educational/inspirational tone — not in sales pitch captions.
